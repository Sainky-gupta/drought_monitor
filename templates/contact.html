Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions?

Our model is trained on historical data and achieves high accuracy. However, predictions should be used as guidance alongside local knowledge and expert advice.

How often is the data updated?

The system uses the latest available satellite data. Updates depend on data availability from satellite sources, typically monthly.

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

Please contact us for commercial licensing and usage terms. We support both academic and commercial applications.

How can I integrate this into my system?

We provide API endpoints for integration. Contact us for API documentation and access credentials.
